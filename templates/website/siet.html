About Us:

Satilila Charitable Society was founded in 2001 under the Society Act 1860, by a renowned industrialist and philanthropist, Shri. S. L. Vaswani. Under his visionary leadership, the society has today evolved as a premier group of institutions i.e. Skyline Institute of Engineering and Technology and Skyline Institute of Management and Technology.

The Institute is strategically located near New Delhi, the Capital city of India, in Greater Noida at Plot No.3, Knowledge Park-II situated on an 8 lane Express highway and sprawls on a 13 Acre lush green surroundings. Skyline is a prestigious name in the education sector, known for enhancing its global reputation for influential scholarship and educational experiences that make a difference in the lives of everyone. Through our actions, SIET is known as one of the most distinguished engineering institutes in Northern India. This achievement is no accident. It is the result of experienced faculty, unrivaled infrastructure, sound industry relationships and incredible facilities pursued for a number of decades.


• Sprawling 13-acre lush green campus

• Approved by AICTE, Ministry of HRD, New Delhi
